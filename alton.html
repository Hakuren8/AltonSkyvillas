About Alton Skyvillas

Nestled in the vibrant neighborhood of Jelutong, Penang. Alton Skyvillas offers the perfect blend of urban convenience and serene living. Residents enjoy easy access to key amenities, including shopping malls, schools, healthcare facilities, and local eateries, all while being surrounded by the island's natural beauty. With its strategic location, you're minutes away from George Town's bustling attractions and Penang Bridge, ensuring seamless connectivity to the mainland. Alton Skyvillas is the ideal choice for those seeking a modern lifestyle in a well-connected, thriving community.
